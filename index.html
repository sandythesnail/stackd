The Method

Every lesson follows the same proven loop

01

Hook

A relatable campus scenario pulls you in before a single concept is introduced.

"Your first paycheck is $47 less than you expected. Why?"
02

Learn

Short, scannable content explains the concept. Target: under five minutes per lesson.

No textbook walls. Interactive, visual, direct.
03

Apply

Make real financial decisions in a simulated scenario with meaningful consequences.

Multiple choice and decision trees grounded in real numbers.
04

Reward

Earn XP, unlock badges, and extend your streak. Every lesson advances your standing.

Progress is visible, tangible, and motivating.
